Bacon

It tastes savory and is very crispy

How to make?

  1. Grab a frying pan
  2. Add oil
  3. Preheat pan
  4. Thaw out the bacon
  5. Fry the bacon
  6. Dry off the bacon
  7. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Pork
  • Salt
  • Smoker

I like bacon and I do recommend it, however it isn't the best health wise so don't eat it too often
